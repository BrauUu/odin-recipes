Burger

Burger patty recipe uses ground beef and an easy bread crumb mixture. Nothing beats a simple hamburger on a warm summer evening!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ground beef
  • Cheese
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Prepare the patty
  2. Roast the patty
  3. Add cheese over the patty
  4. Fry the bacon
  5. Put everything together
